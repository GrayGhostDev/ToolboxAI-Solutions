    Getting Started¶

    Creating Your Account¶

    1. Open the platform by launching the application or visiting the website.
    2. Click on the "Sign Up" button.
    3. Fill in your information:
    4. Username (this will be visible to others)
    5. Email address
    6. Password (must be at least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols)
    7. Display name (optional)
    8. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
    9. Click "Create Account."
    10. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your email.

    Vision: Roblox Educational Platform Transformation¶

    ToolBoxAI-Solutions gives students a gamified, immersive way to engage with curriculum in interactive 3D Roblox environments.

    Core Features for Students¶

    • Explore school-based hubs and classrooms
    • Interact with NPCs and classmates
    • Earn XP, badges, and rewards
    • Take quizzes and assignments
    • Accessible on mobile devices and with screen readers

    First-Time Login¶

    After verifying your email, log in using your username and password. You'll be guided through an onboarding process:

    1. Profile Setup: Upload a profile picture and complete your profile information.
    2. Interest Selection: Choose subjects that interest you for personalized course recommendations.
    3. Learning Goals: Set your learning goals and preferred study schedule.
    4. Platform Tour: Follow the interactive tour highlighting key features.

    Dashboard Navigation¶

    The Student Dashboard is your home base for all learning activities.

    Main Dashboard Elements¶

    1. Navigation Menu: Access different sections of the platform.
    2. Profile Summary: View your level, XP, and quick stats.
    3. Enrolled Courses: See your active courses with progress indicators.
    4. Notifications: View announcements, assignment due dates, and updates.
    5. Learning Progress: Track your overall progress and achievements.
    6. Upcoming Tasks: See upcoming assignments, quizzes, and deadlines.

    Quick Navigation Tips¶

    • Use the search bar to quickly find courses, lessons, or resources.
    • The hamburger menu (☰) can be used to collapse the navigation panel for more screen space.
    • Access your profile settings by clicking on your profile picture.
    • The notification bell shows important updates – click it to see all notifications.

    Course Enrollment¶

    Finding Courses¶

    1. Click on "Courses" in the navigation menu.
    2. Browse available courses by:
    3. Categories: Filter by subject area
    4. Difficulty: Filter by beginner, intermediate, or advanced levels
    5. Duration: Filter by estimated completion time
    6. Ratings: Filter by student ratings

    Enrolling in a Course¶

    1. Click on a course to view its details page.
    2. Review the course information, including:
    3. Course description
    4. Learning objectives
    5. Prerequisites
    6. Syllabus
    7. Educator information
    8. Student reviews
    9. Click the "Enroll" button to add the course to your dashboard.
    10. Some courses may require:
    11. Completion of prerequisites
    12. Parent approval (for younger students)
    13. Payment or subscription (for premium courses)

    Course Management¶

    To manage your enrolled courses:

    1. Go to "My Courses" in the navigation menu.
    2. View all your enrolled courses.
    3. Click the three-dot menu (⋮) on any course card to:
    4. Mark as favorite
    5. Hide from dashboard
    6. Unenroll (note: progress may be lost)
    7. Share with friends

    Accessing Lessons¶

    Lesson Navigation¶

    Once enrolled in a course:

    1. Click on the course from your dashboard or "My Courses" section.
    2. The course page shows all available lessons in sequence.
    3. Lessons are typically organized into modules or sections.
    4. Click on any lesson to begin or continue learning.

    Lesson Interface¶

    The lesson interface includes:

    1. Progress Bar: Shows your position in the current lesson and course.
    2. Content Area: Displays the lesson content (text, images, videos).
    3. Navigation Controls: Move between lessons or sections.
    4. Interactive Elements: Engage with exercises, polls, and practice problems.
    5. Notes Tool: Take notes while learning.
    6. Resource Panel: Access additional materials related to the lesson.

    Types of Lesson Content¶

    Lessons may include various content types:

    • Video Lectures: Watch instructional videos with playback controls
    • Text Content: Read educational material with images and diagrams
    • Interactive Simulations: Engage with hands-on learning activities
    • Practice Exercises: Test your understanding with ungraded exercises
    • Discussion Prompts: Participate in guided discussions

    Completing Lessons¶

    To complete a lesson:

    1. Work through all the content and interactive elements.
    2. Some lessons require you to pass a short knowledge check.
    3. Click "Mark as Complete" or reach the end of the lesson.
    4. The system will record your progress and unlock the next lesson (if applicable).

    Taking Quizzes¶

    Accessing Quizzes¶

    Quizzes are typically found:

    1. At the end of lessons
    2. At the end of course modules
    3. In the "Assessments" section of a course

    Quiz Interface¶

    The quiz interface includes:

    1. Timer: Shows remaining time (if applicable)
    2. Progress Indicator: Shows which question you're on
    3. Question Display: Shows the current question and options
    4. Navigation Buttons: Move between questions
    5. Submit Button: Finalize your answers

    Quiz Types¶

    You may encounter different types of quizzes:

    • Practice Quizzes: Ungraded, for self-assessment
    • Lesson Quizzes: Must be passed to complete a lesson
    • Module Quizzes: Test knowledge from multiple lessons
    • Final Exams: Comprehensive assessment of the entire course

    Quiz Features¶

    • Most quizzes allow you to:
    • Flag questions for review
    • Navigate freely between questions
    • Change answers until submission
    • See your results immediately after submission

    Quiz Results¶

    After submitting a quiz:

    1. View your score and passing status.
    2. Review correct and incorrect answers (if enabled by the educator).
    3. Read explanations for each question (if provided).
    4. Retake the quiz if allowed and necessary.

    Submitting Assignments¶

    Types of Assignments¶

    The platform supports various assignment types:

    • Text Submissions: Written responses or essays
    • File Uploads: Documents, images, or other files
    • Project Links: URLs to external projects
    • Peer Reviews: Evaluations of other students' work
    • Group Assignments: Collaborative work with other students

    Submitting Your Work¶

    To submit an assignment:

    1. Navigate to the assignment from your course page or dashboard.
    2. Read the instructions and requirements carefully.
    3. Prepare your submission according to the guidelines.
    4. Click "Submit Assignment" and follow the prompts to upload or enter your work.
    5. Confirm your submission.

    Tracking Submission Status¶

    After submitting an assignment:

    1. The status will show as "Submitted."
    2. Once reviewed, it may change to "Graded" or "Needs Revision."
    3. You'll receive a notification when feedback is available.

    Viewing Feedback¶

    To view assignment feedback:

    1. Go to the assignment page.
    2. Click "View Feedback."
    3. Review comments and annotations from your educator.
    4. If revisions are required, follow the instructions to resubmit.

    Tracking Progress¶

    Course Progress¶

    Track your progress within each course:

    1. The course card on your dashboard shows overall completion percentage.
    2. Inside each course, view detailed progress by module and lesson.
    3. Completed items are marked with a checkmark (✓).
    4. Your current position is highlighted.

    Learning Analytics¶

    Access your personal learning analytics:

    1. Click on "My Progress" in the navigation menu.
    2. View data on:
    3. Time spent learning
    4. Completion rates
    5. Quiz performance
    6. Strengths and areas for improvement
    7. Learning patterns and recommendations

    Progress Reports¶

    Generate progress reports:

    1. Go to "My Progress" > "Reports."
    2. Select the courses to include.
    3. Choose the date range.
    4. Click "Generate Report."
    5. Download or share the report as needed.

    Earning Achievements¶

    Achievement System¶

    The platform includes a gamified achievement system to motivate learning:

    1. Experience Points (XP): Earned by completing lessons, quizzes, and assignments.
    2. Levels: Progress through levels as you earn XP.
    3. Badges: Awarded for specific accomplishments.
    4. Achievements: Unlocked by meeting certain criteria.

    Viewing Your Achievements¶

    To view your achievements:

    1. Click on your profile picture.
    2. Select "Achievements."
    3. Browse your earned badges and achievements.
    4. View locked achievements and their requirements.

    Types of Achievements¶

    Achievements are organized into categories:

    • Learning Milestones: Completing courses, earning certificates
    • Mastery: Achieving high scores on assessments
    • Consistency: Maintaining learning streaks
    • Exploration: Trying different course categories
    • Community: Participating in discussions and peer reviews

    Redeeming Rewards¶

    Reward System¶

    As you earn XP and achievements, you'll unlock rewards:

    1. Access the Rewards Center from your dashboard or profile menu.
    2. Browse available rewards based on your level and XP.
    3. Use your earned points to redeem rewards.

    Types of Rewards¶

    Available rewards may include:

    • Profile Customization: Avatar items, profile themes, badges
    • Platform Features: Advanced tools, exclusive content
    • Real-World Benefits: Certificates, discounts on premium courses
    • Digital Content: Educational resources, downloadable materials

    Reward Tiers¶

    Rewards are organized into tiers:

    • Basic: Available to all students
    • Bronze: Unlocked at intermediate levels
    • Silver: Unlocked at advanced levels
    • Gold: Unlocked at expert levels and for exceptional achievement

    Communication Tools¶

    Discussion Forums¶

    Participate in course discussions:

    1. Access the discussion forum from the course page.
    2. Browse existing threads or create a new one.
    3. Follow discussion etiquette and guidelines.
    4. Use formatting tools to enhance your posts.

    Direct Messaging¶

    Communicate with educators and peers:

    1. Click on the messaging icon in the navigation menu.
    2. Select a recipient from your contacts.
    3. Compose and send your message.
    4. Attach files if needed (size limits apply).

    Study Groups¶

    Join or create study groups:

    1. Go to "Community" > "Study Groups."
    2. Browse available groups or create your own.
    3. Set group goals and schedules.
    4. Collaborate using group chat and shared resources.

    Account Settings¶

    Profile Settings¶

    Customize your profile:

    1. Click on your profile picture.
    2. Select "Settings."
    3. Update your profile information, including:
    4. Display name
    5. Profile picture
    6. Bio
    7. Learning interests
    8. Social links (optional)

    Privacy Settings¶

    Manage your privacy:

    1. Go to "Settings" > "Privacy."
    2. Control what information is visible to:
    3. Everyone
    4. Enrolled course members
    5. Educators only
    6. No one

    Notification Preferences¶

    Customize your notifications:

    1. Go to "Settings" > "Notifications."
    2. Enable or disable notifications for:
    3. Course announcements
    4. Assignment deadlines
    5. Quiz availability
    6. Feedback received
    7. Discussion replies
    8. Achievement unlocks

    Accessibility Settings¶

    Adjust accessibility features:

    1. Go to "Settings" > "Accessibility."
    2. Customize options such as:
    3. Text size
    4. Color contrast
    5. Screen reader compatibility
    6. Keyboard navigation
    7. Caption preferences

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues¶

    Cannot Access a Course¶

    • Check your internet connection
    • Verify your enrollment status
    • Clear your browser cache or restart the app
    • Contact support if the issue persists

    Quiz or Assignment Won't Submit¶

    • Check your internet connection
    • Take screenshots of your answers as backup
    • Try using a different browser or device
    • Contact support immediately

    Content Not Loading¶

    • Check your internet connection
    • Try refreshing the page
    • Clear your browser cache
    • Update your app if using the mobile version

    Getting Help¶

    If you encounter any issues:

    1. Check the FAQ section accessed via the help icon (?)
    2. Use the in-app chat support
    3. Email support at support@educational-platform.example.com
    4. Call the support line at 1-800-LEARN (available Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm)

    Remember to include:

    • Your username
    • Device and browser information
    • Screenshots of any error messages
    • Step-by-step description of what happened
