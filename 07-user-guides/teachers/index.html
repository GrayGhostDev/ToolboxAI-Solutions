    AI Content Creation¶

    The AI Content Generator¶

    How It Works¶

    Our AI system, powered by Claude and OpenAI GPT models, transforms your educational ideas into immersive 3D Roblox experiences. The process involves several specialized AI agents working together:

    1. Lesson Analysis Agent: Understands your educational objectives
    2. Environment Agent: Designs appropriate 3D spaces
    3. Object Agent: Creates interactive educational elements
    4. Script Agent: Generates game mechanics and interactions
    5. Validation Agent: Ensures educational appropriateness and effectiveness

    Creating Your First Lesson¶

    Step 1: Start with Learning Objectives 

    Example Input:
"Create a 5th grade lesson about the solar system where students can explore planets and learn about their characteristics, orbital patterns, and relative sizes."

Learning Objectives:
- Identify the 8 planets in order from the sun
- Compare planet sizes and characteristics
- Understand orbital periods and distances
- Recognize the concept of scale in space

    Step 2: Provide Context and Requirements - Subject Area: Science, Mathematics, History, Language Arts, etc. - Grade Level: K-12 specification - Duration: Recommended lesson length (15-60 minutes) - Difficulty: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced - Prerequisites: Required prior knowledge - Special Needs: Accommodations for diverse learners

    Step 3: Customize the Experience - Environment Type: Space station, classroom, historical setting, laboratory, etc. - Interaction Style: Exploration, problem-solving, collaboration, experimentation - Assessment Integration: Embedded quizzes, challenges, or projects - Multimedia Elements: Videos, audio narration, interactive displays

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues and Solutions¶

    Lesson Creation Problems¶

    Issue: "AI generation failed or timed out" Causes: - Internet connection instability - Overly complex lesson requirements - Peak usage times causing delays

    Solutions: 1. Check your internet connection stability 2. Simplify your lesson objectives and try again 3. Break complex lessons into smaller parts 4. Try again during off-peak hours 5. Contact support if problem persists

    Issue: "Generated content doesn't match my expectations" Solutions: 1. Refine Your Prompt: Be more specific about your requirements 2. Use Examples: Provide sample activities or reference materials 3. Iterate: Generate multiple versions and combine the best elements 4. Manual Editing: Use our editing tools to customize AI output 5. Template Starting Point: Begin with a pre-made template

    Student Access Issues¶

    Issue: "Students can't join my Roblox experience" Checklist: - [ ] Verify your class code is active and not expired - [ ] Check that students have linked their Roblox accounts - [ ] Ensure students' Roblox privacy settings allow joining experiences - [ ] Confirm your lesson is published and not in draft mode - [ ] Test the experience yourself before student access

    Issue: "Students report lag or poor performance in Roblox" Solutions: 1. Check System Requirements: Ensure students meet minimum specs 2. Reduce Graphics Settings: Lower quality for better performance 3. Limit Concurrent Users: Reduce class size for the session 4. Network Assessment: Test school internet bandwidth 5. Alternative Access: Consider home access for bandwidth-heavy content

    Progress Tracking Issues¶

    Issue: "Student progress not updating in real-time" Troubleshooting Steps: 1. Refresh your dashboard to see if data appears 2. Check that students are properly logged into both platforms 3. Verify Roblox-ToolBoxAI connection is active 4. Look for any error messages in the student experience 5. Contact technical support with specific student and time information
