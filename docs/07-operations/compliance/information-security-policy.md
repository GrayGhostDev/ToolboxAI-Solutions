# Information Security Policy

## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose
This Information Security Policy establishes the framework for managing information security risks and protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets within ToolboxAI Solutions.

### 1.2 Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third-party service providers
- All information systems, networks, and data processing facilities
- All information assets owned, leased, or under the control of ToolboxAI Solutions
- All business processes and activities that involve information processing

### 1.3 Information Security Management System (ISMS)
This policy is part of our ISMS, which is designed to:
- Protect student and educational data
- Ensure compliance with applicable regulations (COPPA, FERPA, GDPR)
- Maintain business continuity
- Protect intellectual property
- Ensure regulatory compliance

## 2. Information Security Objectives

### 2.1 Primary Objectives
- **Confidentiality**: Protect sensitive information from unauthorized access
- **Integrity**: Ensure information accuracy and completeness
- **Availability**: Maintain reliable access to information systems
- **Compliance**: Meet all applicable regulatory requirements
- **Risk Management**: Identify and mitigate information security risks

### 2.2 Specific Goals
- Zero critical security incidents per quarter
- 100% compliance with educational privacy regulations
- 99.9% system availability
- 100% staff security awareness training completion
- 24-hour incident response time for critical issues

## 3. Information Classification

### 3.1 Classification Levels

#### Public
- Information that can be freely shared
- Marketing materials, public documentation
- No special handling requirements

#### Internal
- Information for internal use only
- Business processes, internal communications
- Access restricted to employees and authorized contractors

#### Confidential
- Sensitive business information
- Financial data, strategic plans
- Access restricted to authorized personnel with business need

#### Restricted
- Highly sensitive information
- Student data, personal information
- Access restricted to specific authorized personnel
- Additional security controls required

### 3.2 Student Data Classification
All student data is classified as **RESTRICTED** and requires:
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Access logging and monitoring
- Regular security assessments
- Compliance with COPPA, FERPA, and state privacy laws

## 4. Information Security Controls

### 4.1 Organizational Controls

#### 4.1.1 Information Security Roles and Responsibilities
- **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)**: Overall security strategy and governance
- **Data Protection Officer (DPO)**: Privacy compliance and data protection
- **Security Team**: Implementation and monitoring of security controls
- **All Employees**: Compliance with security policies and procedures

#### 4.1.2 Information Security Committee
- Quarterly meetings to review security posture
- Risk assessment and treatment decisions
- Policy updates and approvals
- Incident response coordination

### 4.2 People Controls

#### 4.2.1 Background Checks
- All employees undergo background checks
- Enhanced checks for positions with access to sensitive data
- Regular re-screening for high-risk positions

#### 4.2.2 Security Awareness Training
- Annual mandatory security training for all employees
- Role-specific training for technical staff
- Regular security awareness communications
- Phishing simulation exercises

#### 4.2.3 Disciplinary Process
- Clear disciplinary procedures for security violations
- Progressive disciplinary actions
- Termination for serious security breaches

### 4.3 Physical Controls

#### 4.3.1 Data Center Security
- 24/7 physical security monitoring
- Biometric access controls
- Environmental monitoring and controls
- Fire suppression and power backup systems

#### 4.3.2 Office Security
- Access control systems
- Visitor management procedures
- Clean desk policy enforcement
- Secure disposal of sensitive materials

### 4.4 Technological Controls

#### 4.4.1 Network Security
- Firewall protection and monitoring
- Intrusion detection and prevention systems
- Network segmentation
- Regular security assessments

#### 4.4.2 Endpoint Security
- Antivirus and anti-malware protection
- Endpoint detection and response (EDR)
- Device encryption
- Regular security updates

#### 4.4.3 Data Protection
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Data loss prevention (DLP) systems
- Regular data backups
- Secure data disposal procedures

## 5. Risk Management

### 5.1 Risk Assessment Process
- Annual comprehensive risk assessments
- Quarterly risk reviews
- Continuous risk monitoring
- Risk treatment plan development

### 5.2 Risk Treatment Options
- **Accept**: Accept the risk with management approval
- **Avoid**: Eliminate the risk by avoiding the activity
- **Mitigate**: Implement controls to reduce the risk
- **Transfer**: Transfer the risk through insurance or contracts

### 5.3 Risk Monitoring
- Regular risk register updates
- Key risk indicator monitoring
- Risk treatment plan tracking
- Management reporting

## 6. Incident Management

### 6.1 Incident Response Team
- CISO as incident commander
- Technical response team
- Communications team
- Legal and compliance team

### 6.2 Incident Classification
- **Critical**: Immediate threat to business operations
- **High**: Significant impact on business operations
- **Medium**: Moderate impact on business operations
- **Low**: Minimal impact on business operations

### 6.3 Response Procedures
- Immediate containment and assessment
- Evidence collection and preservation
- Notification and communication
- Recovery and lessons learned
