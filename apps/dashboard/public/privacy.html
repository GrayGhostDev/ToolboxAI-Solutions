Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-20

ToolBoxAI Solutions (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, including students, parents/guardians, teachers, and administrators. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information in connection with our educational platform and Roblox integrations.

Key Points

  • We collect the minimum data necessary to provide educational services.
  • Student data is processed in accordance with COPPA, FERPA, and applicable privacy laws.
  • We do not sell student personal information.
  • We provide parental consent and data subject rights workflows (access, export, deletion).

Information We Collect

  • Account information (e.g., username, role, school/district)
  • Learning data (e.g., progress, assessments, activities)
  • Technical data (e.g., device, IP address, logs) for security and operations

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the platform and content
  • Support educational analytics and reporting
  • Ensure security, integrity, and compliance

Children’s Privacy and Parental Consent

For users under 13, we obtain verifiable parental consent or ensure that the school consents under FERPA’s school official exception. Parents/guardians can review, export, and delete their child’s data by contacting support or using in-product workflows.

Data Subject Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, export, correct, or delete your data. Request these through in-app Privacy options or contact privacy@toolboxai.com.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security controls, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, monitoring, and incident response processes.
