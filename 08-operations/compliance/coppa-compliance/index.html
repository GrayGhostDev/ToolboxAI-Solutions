    COPPA Compliance Documentation¶

    1. COPPA Overview¶

    1.1 What is COPPA?¶

    The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) is a federal law that protects the privacy of children under 13 years of age. COPPA requires operators of websites and online services to obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting, using, or disclosing personal information from children under 13.

    1.2 COPPA Requirements¶

    • Verifiable Parental Consent: Must obtain consent before collecting data
    • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary information
    • Data Security: Implement reasonable security measures
    • Data Retention: Retain data only as long as necessary
    • Parental Rights: Provide parents with access, deletion, and opt-out rights
    • Privacy Notice: Clear, understandable privacy notices

    1.3 Applicability to ToolboxAI Solutions¶

    Our platform serves educational institutions and may collect data from children under 13, making COPPA compliance essential for: - Student accounts and profiles - Educational progress tracking - Learning analytics and assessments - Communication features - Roblox integration and gameplay data

    3. Data Collection Practices¶

    3.1 Data We Collect from Children Under 13¶

    3.1.1 Directly Collected Information¶

    • Name: First and last name (with parental consent)
    • Email: Parent's email address for consent and communication
    • Age: Birth date to verify age and parental consent
    • School Information: School name and grade level
    • Educational Progress: Learning progress and achievements
    • Gameplay Data: Roblox gameplay and interaction data

    3.1.2 Automatically Collected Information¶

    • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser
    • Usage Data: Time spent, features used, learning activities
    • Location Data: General location (city, state) for educational purposes
    • Cookies: Essential cookies for functionality and security

    3.2 Data We Do NOT Collect¶

    • Precise Location: GPS coordinates or exact location
    • Biometric Data: Fingerprints, facial recognition, voice prints
    • Health Information: Medical conditions or health data
    • Financial Information: Credit card numbers or payment data
    • Social Media: Social media profiles or connections

    3.3 Data Collection Purposes¶

    • Educational Services: Providing personalized learning experiences
    • Progress Tracking: Monitoring and reporting learning progress
    • Safety and Security: Ensuring platform safety and security
    • Communication: Sending educational updates and notifications
    • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

    4. Parental Consent Management¶

    4.1 Consent Collection Methods¶

    4.1.1 Verifiable Parental Consent¶

    We use multiple methods to obtain verifiable parental consent:

    Email Verification: - Send consent request to parent's email - Require parent to click verification link - Confirm parent's identity through additional verification

    Phone Verification: - Call parent's phone number - Verify identity through security questions - Confirm consent verbally and in writing

    Video Call Verification: - Schedule video call with parent - Verify identity with government-issued ID - Record consent confirmation

    Digital Signature: - Electronic signature with timestamp - IP address and device fingerprinting - Multi-factor authentication

    4.1.2 Consent Documentation¶

    • Consent Records: Detailed records of all consent obtained
    • Verification Methods: Documentation of verification methods used
    • Consent Timestamps: When consent was obtained
    • Parent Contact Information: How to reach parents
    • Consent Withdrawal: Process for withdrawing consent

    4.2 Consent Management System¶

    4.2.1 Consent Database¶

    • Parent Information: Contact details and verification status
    • Child Information: Limited to necessary data
    • Consent Status: Current consent status and expiration
    • Consent History: Complete history of consent changes

    4.2.2 Consent Workflow¶

    1. Initial Contact: Reach out to parent for consent
    2. Identity Verification: Verify parent's identity
    3. Consent Collection: Obtain verifiable consent
    4. Documentation: Record consent details
